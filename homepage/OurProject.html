Nova's Adventure Across Canada

Our Project

I think we can all agree that 2020 has been a very weird year. The pandemic caused everyone to experience drastic changes in their lives and plans for the forseeable futures. All of our plans for summer 2020 being cancelled, Nova and I decided to make the best out of the situation and to take the time to explore our home-country, Canada. That's how our adventure across Canada started!

We asked ourselves how we could make this long trip as cheap and comfortable as possible. We thought that camping in a tent for over a month just was not an option, so we came up with the idea of removing the backseat and build a bed platform so we could sleep in the car! We had two big water jugs in the trunk as well as two coolers to keep our food under the bed. The set up turned out to be suprisingly comfortable.
